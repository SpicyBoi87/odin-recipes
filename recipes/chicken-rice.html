Chicken and Rice

A delicious savory meal packed full with flavour, veggies and protein. Just follow these easy steps to make yourself this scrumptious meal.

Ingredients:

  • 250g of chicken - one chicken breast
  • 75g of uncooked white rice
  • One whole brown onion

Steps:

  1. Pour 400ml of water in to a large saucepan and bring to the boil
  2. Add rice to saucepan and cook until rice is soft and fluffy
  3. Fry chicken in a pan until golden brown and cooked all the way through
  4. Add cooked rice to pan with chicken and chopped onion
  5. Serve chicken and rice in a bowl and enjoy!
