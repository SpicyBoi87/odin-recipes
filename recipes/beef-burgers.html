Chicken and Rice

A delicious savory meal packed full with flavour and protein. Just follow these easy steps to make yourself this scrumptious meal.

Ingredients:

  • 250g of 20% mince beef
  • One brioche burger bun
  • One whole brown onion

Steps:

  1. Chop onion and sprinkle into mince in a glass bowl and combine
  2. Roll out two even sized burger patties
  3. Add one tbsp oil to pan and fry turning over until cooked throughout
  4. Serve burgers on the bun with sauce and cheese and enjoy!
